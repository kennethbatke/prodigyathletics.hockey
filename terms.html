[ One-on-One Coaching only ] — applies exclusively to clients enrolled in a personalized one-on-one coaching program
[ Group Coaching only ] — applies exclusively to clients enrolled in a group coaching program
[ Not included with Pre-Made Training Plans ] — does not apply to clients on the self-directed, pre-made training plan tier

Where no tier label appears, the term applies to all clients regardless of program tier.

1. Engagement and Minimum Term

Client agrees to enroll in an online hockey performance training program operated by Prodigy Athletics.

Pre-Made Training Plans

The minimum commitment is one week from the Effective Date. This is a fixed term engagement. No pauses, deferrals, or suspensions are permitted for any reason, including injury, travel, illness, scheduling conflicts, or lack of participation.

Client agrees that the one week minimum term constitutes a binding financial commitment and that all fees for the full term are owed regardless of participation, usage, or results.

Not included with Pre-Made Training Plans: extended minimum term, installment structures, or liquidated damages provisions. See Section 5 for Pre-Made Training Plan refund terms.

One-on-One and Group Coaching Programs

The minimum commitment is twelve weeks from the Effective Date. This is a fixed term engagement. No pauses, deferrals, or suspensions are permitted for any reason, including injury, travel, illness, scheduling conflicts, or lack of participation.

Client agrees that the twelve week minimum term constitutes a binding financial commitment and that all fees for the full term are owed regardless of participation, usage, or results.

[ One-on-One Coaching only / Group Coaching only ]

2. Scope of Services

Services may include but are not limited to:

  • Access to the Prodigy Athletics training application
  • Individualized or pre-designed training programs and schedules
  • Nutrition guidance, meal plans, and meal planning tools
  • One-on-one coaching calls, if included as part of the selected package [ One-on-One Coaching only ]
  • Group calls, community access, and educational resources, if included as part of the selected package [ Group Coaching and One-on-One Coaching only ]

Client understands that one-on-one calls are available but not mandatory unless explicitly included. Prodigy Athletics cannot and does not force Client to book calls, complete workouts, or log nutrition. Results depend on Client participation, effort, and consistency.

Failure to engage does not excuse payment obligations.

Prodigy Athletics does not act as an employer, medical provider, or agent of the Client and does not supervise training in person.

3. Client Responsibilities

Client agrees to:

  • Download and maintain access to the training application
  • Complete scheduled training sessions to the best of their ability
  • Log nutrition and training data accurately where applicable
  • Schedule and attend one-on-one calls if included [ One-on-One Coaching only ]
  • Follow all safety instructions and medical guidance

Client acknowledges that coaching is collaborative and that Prodigy Athletics does not guarantee results.

4. Fees and Payment Terms

Pre-Made Training Plans

Client agrees to pay all fees associated with the selected program. Payments are made weekly. Any failed payment will result in immediate suspension of access to the training application and all associated materials until payment is resolved.

Missed or failed payments do not cancel this Agreement. Prodigy Athletics may pursue collection of any outstanding balance without further notice.

Not included with Pre-Made Training Plans: installment arrangements or extended payment structures.

One-on-One and Group Coaching Programs

Client agrees to pay all fees associated with the selected program. Payment may be made in full or through an approved installment arrangement including every two or four weeks.

All installment arrangements are payment accommodations only and do not alter the total amount owed. Client remains responsible for the full program fee even if an installment plan is selected.

Regardless of payment structure, the full program price is earned over the twelve week minimum term.

If any payment fails, Prodigy Athletics may immediately suspend access to all services including app access, calls, and materials until payment is resolved.

Missed or failed payments do not cancel this Agreement. Prodigy Athletics may pursue collection of any outstanding balance without further notice.

[ One-on-One Coaching only / Group Coaching only ]

5. Onboarding Package

Upon enrollment, One-on-One and Group Coaching clients will receive a physical onboarding package valued at two hundred dollars.

If Client requests termination within seven days, the onboarding package may be returned unused at Client expense. No refund will be issued unless the package is returned.

After seven days, the onboarding package is deemed fully earned and non-refundable.

[ One-on-One Coaching only / Group Coaching only. Not included with Pre-Made Training Plans. ]

6. Refund Policy and Liquidated Damages

Pre-Made Training Plans

There is a zero refund policy for Pre-Made Training Plan subscriptions under any circumstances. No refunds, credits, or prorated adjustments will be issued.

Not included with Pre-Made Training Plans: seven day cancellation window or liquidated damages provisions.

One-on-One and Group Coaching Programs

Client has seven days from the Effective Date to request termination in writing.

If termination occurs within seven days and the onboarding package is returned, Client will owe no further payments and access will be revoked.

If termination occurs after seven days, Client acknowledges that services are customized and front-loaded, including onboarding review, program design, scheduling allocation, and fulfillment of a physical onboarding package valued at two hundred dollars. Capacity is limited and reserved, and Prodigy Athletics incurs substantial non-recoverable costs immediately upon enrollment.

Accordingly, Client agrees that fifty percent of the remaining unpaid balance shall become immediately due as liquidated damages, representing a reasonable estimate of Prodigy Athletics' losses and not a penalty.

No credits or prorated adjustments will be issued after seven days.

[ One-on-One Coaching only / Group Coaching only ]
