Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026

Prodigy Athletics ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you interact with our website, lead capture forms, and SMS communications.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Contact information: name, phone number, and email address provided through our lead capture forms or direct inquiries.
  • Program information: details you share about your athlete's age, level of play, goals, and development needs.
  • Communications: messages exchanged via SMS, email, or other channels.
  • Usage data: standard website analytics data such as pages visited and time on site. We do not collect personally identifiable information through analytics without your consent.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Follow up with you via SMS, email, or phone regarding hockey development coaching services.
  • Personalize our outreach based on the information you have shared about your athlete's goals and needs.
  • Schedule and manage discovery calls and coaching engagements.
  • Improve our programs and communication based on feedback.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

3. SMS Communications

By submitting a lead capture form and providing your phone number with SMS consent, you agree to receive SMS text messages from Prodigy Athletics regarding hockey development coaching services. This includes follow-up messages, booking reminders, and coaching program information.

  • Message and data rates may apply.
  • Message frequency varies based on your interaction with our program.
  • Reply STOP at any time to opt out of SMS messages.
  • Reply HELP for assistance.
  • Consent to SMS communications is not a condition of purchasing any service.

We will not share your phone number or SMS consent with any third party for their own marketing purposes.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: We use platforms such as Twilio (SMS delivery), Meta (lead ads), and Anthropic-powered tools to manage communications. These providers process data solely to deliver services on our behalf and are bound by confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information when required by law or to protect our legal rights.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services, fulfill the purposes described in this policy, or as required by law. If you opt out of SMS communications, we will retain a record of your opt-out to ensure we do not contact you again.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal obligations.
  • Opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP.
  • Opt out of email communications by following the unsubscribe link in any email.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at kenneth.batke@gmail.com.

7. Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use standard analytics tools to understand how visitors use the site. This data is aggregated and does not identify individual users. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

9. Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of our services after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
