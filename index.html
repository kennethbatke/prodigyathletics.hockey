Frequently Asked Questions

How does online coaching actually work?
Prodigy operates through high touch support that includes weekly video check-ins, personalized training and recovery plans, video breakdowns of your gameplay and skills, and constant communication via text and voice messaging. Everything is tailored to your schedule, goals, and development needs.
Will this work for my athlete?
Prodigy works for committed athletes who are willing to learn, grow, and implement feedback. If your athlete is open to honest guidance, accountable to their goals, and ready to put in the work, this program will help them develop confidence, consistency, and leadership.
What if my athlete struggles with inconsistency, fear of making mistakes, or confidence dips?
This is exactly what Prodigy specializes in. Understanding the root of inconsistency, overcoming the fear of mistakes, and building lasting confidence are core pillars of our mentorship. We help athletes develop the mental tools to perform freely and consistently.
What ages is this for?
Prodigy is designed for hockey athletes ages 12 and up who are committed to their development and ready to grow as both players and people.
What is Phase 1?
Phase 1 is a fully personalized 3 month foundation that includes personalized training plans, nutrition and recovery support, schedule management, weekly check-ins, ongoing messaging support, skill and gameplay analysis, leadership and confidence coaching, and support through challenge moments.
How much support does my athlete get?
Athletes receive high touch communication throughout the week with ongoing text and voice messaging support plus structured weekly check-ins. You are never left waiting. Support is consistent and accessible.
How much does it cost?
Prodigy is an investment designed for serious families who want the highest level of personal mentorship. We walk through the details on your call to ensure the program is the right fit for your athlete and your family.
What if my athlete is injured?
Injuries are part of sports, and we are here to help navigate them. We provide recovery planning, confidence rebuilding during time off, and structured return-to-play support to ensure your athlete comes back stronger mentally and physically.
