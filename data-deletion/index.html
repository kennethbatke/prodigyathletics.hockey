Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: June 2026

If you have connected your Facebook or Instagram account to Prodigy Athletics through our lead capture forms or messaging tools, you may request deletion of any personal data we have collected about you.

How to Request Data Deletion

To request deletion of your data, follow one of these methods:

1
Email us directly at support@prodigyathletics.ca with the subject line "Data Deletion Request." Include the name and email address associated with your account or inquiry. We will process your request within 30 days.
2
Via Facebook: Go to Facebook Settings → Your Facebook Information → Off-Facebook Activity → Clear History. This removes activity data Facebook has shared with third-party apps including ours.
3
Via Instagram: Go to Instagram Settings → Security → Apps and Websites → find Prodigy Athletics → Remove. This revokes access and we will delete associated data within 30 days.

What Data We Hold

When you interact with our Facebook Page or Instagram account, we may hold: your name, contact information, and messages you have sent. We do not sell this data to third parties.

Response Timeline

We will confirm receipt of your deletion request within 5 business days and complete deletion within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email when complete.

Questions

For any questions about your data or this process, contact us at support@prodigyathletics.ca.
